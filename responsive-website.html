Responsive Website: Pixel Art

Link to the Website

This was my first encounter to the world of Multimedia Designers. It was a big challenge since everything was completely new for me. The result was quite pleasing back then, though now with my current knowledge and abilities I would do it in a completely different way.

The website has one break point at 30em, making it suitable for phone screens and tablets. For the palette of the website I chose bright and vivid secondary and tertiary colors. I found appropriate the combination of light colors contrasted by some strong ones. In terms of font for my website I chose the sans-serif Corbel. While still being fully legible and readable parts of the numbers go bellow the baseline giving it a playful and artistic look. Being A-symmetrical the column grid in the tablet version with two uneven in size columns makes the design more appealing to the eye. Another design principles that are found on the website are consistency and similarity.
