Me, Elitsa

My name is Elitsa Raycheva. I am a 19-year-old Bulgarian girl. I grew up in the beautiful city of Sofia, historical and cultural center since ancient times, situated in the foot of the majestic Vitosha Mountain. Though my country will always be first in my heart I cannot deny I adore travelling. For my 19 years I’ve aready been to 3 different continents and 17 countries. But my goal is to multiply this number. I want to explore the human jungle of a busy metropolis as well as a peaceful remote corner of the world where you just stand and watch in awe the greatness of nature.

In August last summer I moved to Copenhagen to persue my dream of becoming a web-designer. Right now I am a 1st semester Multimedia student at KEA (hopefully soon 2nd semester). I have passion for design, photography and photo editing. Creative ideas have haunted my mind and I can’t wait to make them happen.
