Interactive Animation: Billy the silly ghost

Link to the Interactive Animation

This is the continuation of the previous module where we had to come up with a short story. This time our task was to code a short interactive scene from our story. Even though the story about Billy the silly ghost looks quite simple at first glance coding it turns out to be far more complicate. For this reason there slight changes in the setting and plot. Instead of the evil ghost appearing and getting into the pumpkin it’s a lightning during a thunderstorm that hits the orange fruit.

For this seemingly simple scene it takes numbers of JavaScript functions to make it happen – some random, some following one after another, some upon interaction. I find this submission one of the most challenging during the semester. But as we all know smooth seas don’t make skilled sailors. And JavaScript is a hell of a stormy ocean – but once you make it work you feel endless joy.
