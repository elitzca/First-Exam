Sprite Sheet Animation: Billy the silly ghost

Link to the Sprite Sheet Animation

In this theme I was introduced to the basics of story-telling, setting, character design, narrative curve and animation principles. I marked significant improvement in working with Adobe Illustrator. My character – Billy the silly ghost, was inspired from the Pictoplasma style. I decided to go with pastel earth colors for the palette.

I found creating my character a profile with his personal characteristics both physical and psychological very pleasing and exciting activity. It helped me understand my idea more deeply and create a better story. So Billy the silly ghost is a young, calm and peaceful ghost. Unlike others he is good-hearted and hates when people are scared of him. He lives in a quiet small town and loves gardening. Like every ghost his biggest passion is Halloween, but opposite to his spooky and threatening fellows, he adores the holiday because of the decorations, pumpkins and sweets. That’s why Billy takes special care of his Halloween pumpkin. He wants it to grow big and beautiful. Because of his innocence and purity Billy the silly ghost is consisted of oval shapes and lines and is in skyblue colour. Even his name tells us once more about his good-natured even naïve personality. On the other hand the evil ghost is with sharp edges and triangle-like body, white with purple outlining – all visual identity characteristics of antiheroes. When the wicked ghost goes into the pumpkin he gives it his traits.
